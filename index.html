It all started with your first travel. You discovered new cultures, new people and different gastronomy. You are a person who loves travelling, but you like to host travellers from airbnb in order to earn some money too. Imagine you can continue hosting people from airbnb while you are travelling around the world.

What I'm looking for with this project is to interact virtually and physically with people who book a room from airbnb. To do that, we'll use our personal robot host, to open the main door and guide through the house when necessary. The service will include a mobile app to fullfil all the clients requests. It will be possible to interact with suppliers to serve breakfast, lunch or dinner to our customers. Hope you like it :-D
